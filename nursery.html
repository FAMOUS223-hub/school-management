Building Strong Foundations

At Premium Academy Nursery, we believe that the early years are the perfect opportunity to ignite a lifelong love for learning. Our play-based curriculum is carefully crafted to nurture curiosity, creativity, and confidence in every child.

Every activity is designed with intention — from sensory play that builds neural connections to group activities that teach collaboration and empathy. Our dedicated educators guide each child's journey with warmth and expertise.

Outdoor Excellence

World-Class Playground

Our award-winning outdoor play area is designed to inspire adventure, physical development, and social interaction. Spread across 2 acres, it features age-appropriate equipment and natural exploration zones.

  • Adventure climbing structures with safety surfaces
  • Sensory garden with herbs, textures, and scents
  • Covered sand and water play stations
  • Tricycle track with traffic signs for road safety
  • Shaded rest areas with age-appropriate seating
  • Outdoor classroom for nature-based learning
