Our Curriculum

Our play-based curriculum focuses on holistic development, combining sensory exploration, motor skills, and early learning foundations.

🧠

Cognitive Development

Problem-solving, memory games, and basic concepts through interactive play.

💪

Physical Development

Gross and fine motor skills through movement activities and sensory play.

🎨

Creative Expression

Art, music, and imaginative play to foster creativity and self-expression.

🤝

Social Skills

Group activities and peer interaction to build communication and sharing.

🌱

Emotional Growth

Building confidence, independence, and emotional awareness.

📖

Language Development

Storytelling, songs, and basic vocabulary building.
