Junior High School

Preparing Leaders for Tomorrow

Our JHS program is specifically designed to bridge the gap between primary education and senior high school. Students develop advanced critical thinking skills, deeper subject knowledge, and the academic discipline needed for success.

  • Specialized subject teachers for Mathematics, Science, English, and French
  • Career guidance and counseling program starting from JHS 1
  • Leadership training through prefectship and student council
  • Annual academic trips and exchange programs
  • BECE preparation with mock examinations and personalized coaching
  • Digital literacy program with one-device-per-student policy
